Number Translation Service (NTS) adalah istilah yang digunakan untuk aplikasi seperti tolfree, premium rate number, televoting


• Reverse charging
The service subscriber is charged, the calling party is not charged.
• Premium rate charging
The service user (calling party) is charged higher than for normal calls.
• Split charging
The charge is split between calling party and service subscriber.
